Unadjusted stations

Shows the stations that were previously adjusted and got unadjusted because of the current mirroring/unmirroring operation.

This usually is caused by the new position of the mirrored/unmirrored station that makes impossible to solve triangles for other stations that were previously adjusted.

Choose if you want to go on with the mirroring/unmirroring action clicking on the Ok button or cancel it clicking on the Cancel button.
