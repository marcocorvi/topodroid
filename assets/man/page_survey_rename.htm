SURVEY RENAME

This dialog is opened from the Rename menu of the Survey Info window. The menu is present only at "tester" level.

Buttons:

  • Rename the survey
  • Add a prefix to all the survey stations.
  • Close closes the dialog.

Survey rename
The new survey name must be typed in the field to the right of the "Rename" button.
The "section" points have the survey name written in the option "-scrap". The survey name is automatically updated the next time the sketch is opened in TopoDroid.

WARNING. The name of the survey cannot contain special chracters: '/', ':', '<', '>', '*', '\'.
' The name of the survey cannot contain spaces. Spaces are replaced with '_' (underscore).

Station prefix
The prefix for the station names must be typed in the field to the right of the "Prefix" button.
