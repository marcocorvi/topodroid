PHOTO LIST

This dialog is opened from the Photo button in the survey info window, or from the Photo menu in the survey data window.

It display the list of the photos of the survey, both those taken for shots and those taken for sketches.
Each entry has

  • the number of the photo
  • shot to which photo was assigned, if the photo was taken for a shot
  • photo comment

Tapping an entry in the list opens the Photo edit dialog.
