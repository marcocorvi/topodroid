SHOT BUFFER

This dialog is opened from the Recover | Paste menu of the Survey Data window if the shot-buffer is not empty. Shot buffer [T]
The shot buffer is filled with a multishot selection.
Either the shots at the end of the survey are "copied" into the buffer, or selected shots are deleted from the survey and go into the buffer.

The data in the shot-buffer can be copied from the buffer and appended to the survey. With the "move" button the shot-buffer is cleared after the copy.
The shots are appended in the order they are added to the buffer. Alternatively, they can be appended sorted by the index, if the appropriate checkbox is marked.
The shot stations are replaced with new names different from those already in the survey. The graph structure of the shots in the buffer is preserved.
