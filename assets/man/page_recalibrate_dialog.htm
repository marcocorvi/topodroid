Recompute Dialog

The recomputation of the directions of the shots is available only for Cavway data.

With this dialog the user selects the calibration that must be used to recompute the direction of the survey shots.
After having selected the Cavway calibration, press the "OK" button to recompute the shot direction.

There are three entry field for the error thresholds for acceleration, magnetic field, and dip. For shots with differences between the two sensor sets above one threshod, a comment is inserted with the error value.
The defualt thresholds are the values of the accuracy settings.
