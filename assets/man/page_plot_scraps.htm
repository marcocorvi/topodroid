SKETCH SCRAPS

With this dialog you can add a scrap to a sketch or switch among the scraps of a sketch.
It is opened from the Scraps menu of the sketch window.

A sketch can be divided in more scraps.
Only one scrap is active at a time, the others are shown only in outline (the walls).

The New button add a scrap to the sketch. The newly added scrap becomes the active scrap.

With the Next and Prev button you can switch to another scrap, to work on that.
These buttons are greyed out when the active scrap is the last or the first, respectively.

The Back button closes the dialog without doing anything.
