The settings are subdivided in categories.
For each category there is the name of the category followed by the setting in curly braces.
There is one setting per line. Each setting line contains: - the setting key (all uppercase), - the usage profile at whiche the setting has effect, - the setting type with possible constraints, - the title of the setting in double quotes, - a description of the setting in double quotes.
The usage profile are: B (0: basic), N (1: normal) A (2: advanced), E (3: expert), T (4: tester), D (5: developer).
The setting types are: Int, Bool, Float, String, Enum, Button and Color.
Numerical types can be followed by by the interval of allowed values in square brackets (possibly with no upper bound).
Enums are followed by the list of possible values and meanings in curly brackets.
